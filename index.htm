About Us

We’re creating a vibrant collection of 4,444 adorable, doodle-style capybaras, ready to make waves on Cardano!


At our core, we’re a team on a mission to build a fun, inclusive community that shares our love for these gentle giants. Our true focus is finding meaningful ways to reward and engage our holders-through thoughtful perks, engaging experiences for everyone who wants to join the fun.

While we embrace a meme-first vibe, we’re also approaching this project with thoughtful planning. Behind the cozy visuals is a team genuinely committed to building something meaningful - and we’re excited to show you what’s ahead as our roadmap unfolds.

Our vision also goes beyond the blockchain. Long-term, we aim to raise funds not just within Cardano and Web3, but also from external sources to support our holders and give back to real-world animal-focused shelters. While capybaras are our spirit animals, our donation efforts will extend to a wide range of animals in need..


Capybaras aren’t just the world’s largest rodents—they’re also some of the chillest, most lovable creatures out there. Their peaceful, social nature inspires everything we do.

Whether you’re here for the art, the community, the rewards, or just some good capybara vibes-there’s a warm spot waiting for you in the Crew.

Let’s build something special - one cozy capybara at a time.
